Four specialized AI agents working together like a real founding team. Each brings unique expertise to help you build a successful startup.

CEO Agent

Vision, Strategy, and Final Decision-Maker

The CEO Agent acts as the leader of the startup. It defines the long-term vision, aligns all other agents, and makes final decisions based on their inputs. It balances growth, risk, and feasibility to guide the startup in the right direction.

What the CEO Agent does:

Defines startup vision and mission
Sets long-term and short-term goals
Evaluates suggestions from other agents
Resolves conflicts between strategies

Marketing Agent

Growth, Branding, and Customer Reach

The Marketing Agent focuses on how people discover, understand, and trust the product. It creates strategies to attract users and build brand awareness.

What the Marketing Agent does:

Identifies the target audience
Suggests marketing channels
Plans campaigns
Improves messaging
Tracks engagement ideas

Finance Agent

Money, Budget, and Sustainability Manager

The Finance Agent evaluates costs, revenue potential, and financial risks. It ensures the startup can survive and grow without running out of money.

What the Finance Agent does:

Estimates expenses
Calculates pricing strategies
Predicts revenue
Checks profitability
Notifies financial risks

Customer Agent

Voice of the User

The Customer Agent represents the end users. It focuses on user needs, expectations, pain points, and satisfaction.

What the Customer Agent does:

Analyzes user needs
Predicts customer reactions
Identifies pain points
Suggests improvements
Measures satisfaction
